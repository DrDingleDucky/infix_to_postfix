# Infix To postfix

Convert infix notation to postfix notation with the shunting yard algorithm.

## Example

```text
input: 1 + 2
output: 1 2 +

input: 34 * 56 - 7
output: 34 56 * 7 -

input: 12 * ( 9 - 1 ) / 4
output: 12 9 1 - * 4 /

input: 10 + 15 * ( 100 - -40 / 10 ) * 2
output: 10 15 100 -40 10 / - * 2 * +
```
